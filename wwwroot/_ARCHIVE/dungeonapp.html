Dungeon App

Over 6 weeks, I learned C#. To put my skills to the test, I was tasked with building a text-based dungeon crawler game.

Instead of the typical heroes-fighting-monsters game, I decided to reverse it. In my game, you can choose from a variety of monsters, and you must clear all of the brave adventurers out of your beloved dungeon.
